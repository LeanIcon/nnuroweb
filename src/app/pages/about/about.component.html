About Us

Medical Supplies/Health Consultancy

Nnuro is your No.1 App and Web platform that aims to simplify and improve the efficiency of the pharmaceutical and medical supply chain, with the aim of ensuring the delivery of quality and affordable drugs and medical supplies. The Nnuro Team has extensive experience in healthcare, management, and technology, which they have marshalled to provide a solution customers will appreciate. Nnuro brings together buyers and sellers of pharmaceutical products and services under an umbrella of trust, technology, and transparency.

Thus, we have created a vibrant community of pharmaceutical and medical suppliers that are eager to embrace the challenges of today’s economic marketplace. We are focused on cost containment and efficiency and hold true to our mission of providing our partners with an advantage over their competition. Nnuro’s flexible marketplace encourages price transparency and access to some of the best retail and wholesale facilities in the nation. Our strategy is to utilize price analytics and supplier independence to increase our competitiveness and pass that advantage directly to our customers. We offer our customers the best pricing and availability of generic and brand pharmaceuticals, medical equipment and supplies within the market place.
